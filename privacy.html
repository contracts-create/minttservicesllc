Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 1, 2026

Overview

Mintt Services LLC ("Mintt Services," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit our website at minttservicesllc.com or contact us for business purposes.

By using our website or submitting information through our contact or RFQ forms, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, title, and contact information (email address, phone number)
  • Organization or agency name
  • Procurement requirements, quantities, delivery timelines, and related details submitted through our RFQ portal
  • Documents uploaded voluntarily through our RFQ portal such as Statements of Work or specifications
  • Communications you send us via email or contact forms

We also automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our site, including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring URLs. This data is used solely for site performance and security monitoring.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to RFQ submissions, quotes, and procurement inquiries
  • Communicate with you about services, capabilities, and teaming opportunities
  • Fulfill contractual obligations for awarded contracts
  • Improve our website and service offerings
  • Comply with applicable federal, state, and local laws and regulations
  • Maintain records required for government contracting compliance

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With vetted suppliers and logistics partners as necessary to fulfill your procurement requirements
  • With government agencies or prime contractors as required by contract performance obligations
  • As required by law, regulation, or valid legal process including federal acquisition regulations
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of Mintt Services LLC or others

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the information we collect. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We encourage you to exercise caution when submitting sensitive information electronically.

For sensitive government requirements or classified information, please contact us directly by phone or encrypted email rather than using our web forms.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, satisfy legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Government contracting records are retained in accordance with applicable federal recordkeeping requirements.

Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information where no legal obligation requires its retention
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to SAM.gov, SBA.gov, and other government or third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies separately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.
